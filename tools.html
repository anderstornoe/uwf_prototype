Uptower Repair

Tools Checklist

Chain Block (3 pcs)

Used for lifting and securing heavy components during the repair process.

Flat Head Screwdriver and Dentist Tool

Used for removal of gaskets and small components within tight spaces.

Lifting Eye for Blade Rod Struts

Lifting point when working on cylinder components such as blade rod struts.

Measuring Tool

Used before and after mounting the piston to ensure correct positioning.

Plastic Container (max height 20 cm)

Used to collect and store parts or fluids during the repair process.

Cleaning Paper/Towels/Mats

For cleaning components and surfaces during the repair process.

Plastic Bag for Dirty Towels/Paper or Trash

For safely storing used towels, papers, and other waste materials.

Nitril and/or Rubber Gloves

To protect hands from contaminants and to maintain a clean working environment.
